Margherita
Classic Margherita Pizza is a perfect amalgamation of cheesy, creamy sauce topped with a melange of spices and herbs. Baked to perfection, this classic Pizza can be prepared easily at home that too without putting in much efforts.

Thin Crust
Garden Vegetable Thin Crust Pizza is for all the veggie lovers. Its thin crust is what sets it apart, it is homemade and as crunchy as it should be. It is full of cheese which melts in your mouth.

Pepperoni Pizza
